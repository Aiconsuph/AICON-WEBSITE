About Us

AICON Construction Supplies is a diversified conglomerate with four core businesses: all types of building materials, from hardware products to cement, gravel, and sands (aggregates), heavy equipment, construction machinery; general trade; procurement of request-specific products; and logistics. AICON is involved in the distribution and procurement of all kinds of construction materials. We ensure quality at a competitive price. With the help of our logistics, we also deliver the materials to the project sites with care so they will be used as new without damaging them. We understand the challenges and needs of transporting building materials from leading manufacturers to construction sites.

Mission

  • To provide professional building material advisory, customer-centric performance, and support to the industry.
  • To develop innovative, high-impact products and solutions that create value for our customers.
  • To provide affordable pricing and top-notch quality service to meet the demands of the market.

Vision

To be the Philippines' Leading Innovative Building Materials Specialist.

Core Values

  • Be the light, stay focused, and uphold integrity.
  • Teamwork Makes Dreams Work Never Give Up!

Board of Directors

Mr. Greggy A. Massido

Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Masiddo is the founder and CEO of Aicon Construction Supplies and General Trading. With a strong background in the construction industry from Bahrain, he has successfully launched Aicon in the Philippines, focusing on high-quality, innovative building materials. His previous roles include project manager at Abu Nasar Construction, marketing and business development manager at Family Home Care Services, and sales consultant at Vulkan Trading Co. WLL. He also has experience in real estate, digital marketing, and nursing, and is a member of Toastmasters International.

Mr. Gregorio E. Masiddo, Jr.

Chief Financial Officer

Engr. Masiddo, a retired Chief of Planning at DPWH Region 4-A, has extensive experience managing diverse government projects, including construction, roadwork, and flood control in Quezon City. He is a former President of the PICE Rizal Chapter and currently serves as Vice President and Chairman of the Infrastructure Committee of BRAI in Cainta Rizal. He also works as a civil engineering consultant for Cainta Municipality and has a strong background in both public and private sector contracting. Engr. Masiddo holds a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from the University of the East and a Master’s in Public Administration from the Philippine College of Health Science.

Eileen Cruz-David

Chief Operating Officer

Ms. David has over a decade of experience in government service, with a strong background in communication and production. At the Bureau of Communications Services (BCS), she excelled in roles such as Production Planning and Control Officer V and Division Chief, leading the Bureau to ISO 9001:2015 certification and supporting major national campaigns. Promoted to Deputy Director-General for Knowledge and Quality Management at the Philippine Information Agency (PIA) in 2023, she managed ISO transitions and gender development projects. Before her government career, Ms. David held academic and administrative roles at Centro Escolar University. She holds a Bachelor's in Psychology and a Master’s in Behavioral Science from CEU, Manila, and is a licensed professional under the Philippine Psychology Act of 2009.
